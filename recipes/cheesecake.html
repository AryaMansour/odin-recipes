Cheesecake Recipe

Description

A delicious cheescake to enjoy with other or on your own!

Ingredients

  • 250ml Milk
  • 950 grams cream cheese
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 cups of sugar

Steps

  1. First you need to beat your cream cheese and sugar on high speed till combined.
  2. Then mix in your eggs 1 at a time and continue to combine with your milk.
  3. Then throw in the oven at 400 degrees for 30-40 minutes, until golden brown.
  4. Your delicious cheesecake is now done and ready for consumption.
